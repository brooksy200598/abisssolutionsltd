Welcome

ABISS Retail Solutions Ltd is a privately owned company with a portfolio of multi-disciplined support services. With a proven track record of combined services in project managament, shopfitting, installation and construction in the retail environment.

ABISS was formed as a direct result of client growth in the showroom sector, now working with blue chip and FMCG retailers. Through an ever changing market, the company has already established a firm platform and base to trade.

Offering our clients committed support in design, planning and implementation of projects from the small to principle contractor support.

ABISS has the ability to react quickly by having our own comprehensive database of trades and installers. Many of the installers are vetted through time served work and having previously been part of the group to manage the full programme of showroom change within a large DIY organisation.

Having recently introduced new components to our growing repertoire of services, including manufacturing, this amalgamation further enhances our ability to provide solutions to all sizes and types of simple to complicated Projects.

Showroom Specialists

ABISS have a proven record in the complicated area of specialised shop fitting of Kitchens, Bathrooms and Bedrooms, with experienced and knowledgeable management through to time served and extremely competent installers and fitters.

Approx. £14m of labour costs installed in the calendar year of 2010.

A management team dedicated to “right first time” with stores as our main focus so speed of implementation as well as quality of installation a priority in the customer proposition, and resolution of issues a major factor in the teams thought process.

Costs reduced on all projects by costing by time rather than the job, multi-skilled workforce and a robust schedule of works.

Further costs reduced by affecting solutions on catchment areas and introducing better controls on travel and accommodation.

Remedial costs generally included in all pricing as is stock consolidation and Project Management.

Reduced timescales of implementation lead to increased sales, less disruption and stock/displays available to trade in stores leaving less discontinued products on displays.

ABISS provided a link into all functions of your business in decision making so the implementation and final customer offer is fully expected, trained and developed by using experienced support managers.

We work really closely with stock vendors and distribution to ensure decisions are also based on the capacity, workloads and process in the implementation.
