About Us - Why Choose ABISS Solutions?

ABISS is a privately owned company with a portfolio of multi-disciplined services and a proven track record of providing combined solutions to projects from audit, project management, shopfitting, installation and joinery in the domestic and commercial markets. ABISS was originally formed in June 2014 as a direct result of growth in the showroom sectors and after working solely with a blue chip retailer, re-established themselves in June 2017 to the domestic and commercial markets. Through our vast experience and knowledge, we have established a firm platform on which to offer support to our clients. We are dedicated to providing turnkey solutions on all our projects, no matter how big or small, through our own professional and experienced teams, backed up where necessary by our support partners.

We offer our clients committed support in the design, planning and execution of all our projects, offering solutions to the most minor problems right through to complete contractor support. ABISS has the ability to react to most requirements through our own comprehensive database of installers and tradepersons. All of our installers have vetted through a national insitute of the industry with many having time served experience of working with ABISS through dozens of program projects within a large DIY organization. Having recently introduced new service components to our repertoire of solutions, including supply and joinery, this further enhances our ability to provide turnkey solutions to the most complicated projects. ABISS recognises that our clients have many options when it comes to service suppliers, however we are not trying to be the same as the rest of the market but stand alone with our unique service offer as we can tailor our support to individual client needs. We see things a bit differently from the main stream and offer packages of services that fully support the client requirements

Compliance

ABISS employ the services of a health and safety (H&S) partner, supplying us with full H&S updates and advisory work. H&S is the primary concern of the company with the general manager responsible for liaising with the H&S partner on critical matters, monthly and quarterly updates, and the compliance of the company.

The GM will brief out H&S via training, email, notive boards and with dedicated training with site specific teams. Management of all risk assessments, method statements, project training, policies and procedures is the responsibility of the GM. Each on-site team will use digital imaging (Capture) to ensure that the location of each project team have fully understood their obligations before commencing any work. Part of H&S is to ensure that robust insurance cover is in place on all activity that we carry out. Our enhanced level of cover allow us to operate within client premises and offers confidence, security and peace of mind that safe operating practices are adhered to.

Public liability - £10 million cover
Employee liability - £10 million cover
Product liability - £5 million cover
Indemnity - £2 million cover

Random audit functions are in place to monitor our process and activity at frequent periods throughout the year.
